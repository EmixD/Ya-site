Ongoing research

Liquid crystals

We investigate the reorientation of the director in NLCs due to the influence of external electric field in various cell configurations. The goal is to obtain analytical expressions for the director distribution in the cell, threshold voltages and typical switch-on and relaxation times while accounting for flexoelectric effects, finite ancoring and easy axes gliding. We are also investigating the SPP propagation on the NLC-metal interface.

Solar physics

We study the physical conditions (temperatures, turbulent velocities and especially magnetic fields) during active processes on the Sun. Within this project we process and analyse the observed spectral line profiles of prominences and flares. Using the unique characteristics of the solar spectrograph we directly compare the I + V to I – V Stokes profiles and calculate the corresponding magnetic field parameters.
